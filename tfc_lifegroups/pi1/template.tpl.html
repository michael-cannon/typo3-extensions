We welcome you to browse through our many different lifegroups at Trinity. We have over four hundred lifegroups ranging from prayer to mountain biking to Bible-studies. We're sure to have one that fits you just right.

You can search by a leader's name, the name of a lifegroup, or just something that you would enjoy doing with people you like.

For example: if you know John Doe and know that he is a leader, just type his name in the search box on the left and you'll find the lifegroup(s) that he leads. Or if you want to find a cooking-based lifegroup, type "cooking" in the search box. You get the idea.

So check your calendars and join a lifegroup. We look forward to seeing you in life.
